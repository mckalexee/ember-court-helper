## Friendship System

Ember Court guests use the WoW **Friend Faction** reputation system. Each guest has an independent friendship level that progresses through six tiers:

| Level | Threshold | Cumulative Points |
|-------|-----------|-------------------|
| Stranger | 0 | 0 |
| Acquaintance | 8,400 | 8,400 |
| Buddy | 8,400 | 16,800 |
| Friend | 8,400 | 25,200 |
| Good Friend | 8,400 | 33,600 |
| Best Friend | 8,400 | 42,000 |

Each level requires 8,400 friendship points to advance to the next. Total to Best Friend: 42,000 points.

### How Friendship Points Are Earned

- Friendship points are gained each time a guest attends an Ember Court session.
- The amount gained scales with the guest's **happiness level** at the end of the event.
- Higher happiness = more friendship points per session.
- The **"Your Reputation Precedes You"** buff provides a 30% permanent friendship increase after reaching Best Friend with any guest.
- Holiday events (Noblegarden's "Theotar's Egg Hunt", Brewfest) can grant +300% friendship bonus.

### Best Friend Benefits

- **Permanent RSVP**: The guest can be invited without needing an RSVP item.
- **Best Friend Quest**: A unique quest chain unlocks upon reaching Best Friend + inviting the guest again.
- **"Friend of a Friend"**: After Best Friend, you can share your progress to boost friendship gains for alts or other players.

## Tribute System

Ember Court rewards come from **tribute chests** that guests leave at the end of each court session. The quality of the tribute depends on the guest's **happiness level**:

| Happiness Level | Tribute Tier | Loot Quality |
|-----------------|--------------|--------------|
| Miserable | No tribute | Nothing |
| Unhappy | Trifling Tribute | Basic (anima, grey items) |
| Uncomfortable | Trifling Tribute | Basic |
| Comfortable | Tribute | Standard (weapons, quest items) |
| Happy | Substantial Tribute | Good (includes rare weapons, pets, cloaks) |
| Elated | Extravagant Tribute | Best (all items in loot table, highest drop rates) |

Higher-tier tributes include all items from lower tiers plus additional rare items. Mounts, pets, and cosmetic cloaks generally require at minimum a Tribute (Comfortable) or better, with best drop rates from Extravagant Tribute (Elated).

## Data Confidence Notes

1. **Friendship level requirements for pets/mounts:** Many sources are based on player reports in Wowhead comments rather than official documentation. Some players report receiving pets at lower friendship levels than expected, suggesting RNG may be involved or thresholds may have been adjusted in patches.

2. **Transmog weapons:** Verified against Wowhead loot table data from tribute objects. All item IDs confirmed via Wowhead database. Most transmog weapons appear to drop regardless of friendship level (available from Stranger onward), with the main factor being happiness/tribute tier.

3. **Shared weapon pools:** Many weapon transmogs are shared across guests of the same covenant faction. Guest-unique weapons are more distinctive and tend to be higher rarity.

4. **Mount drop rates:** The Gruesome Flayedwing appears to have a notably higher drop rate than other covenant feature mounts, with multiple reports of first-visit drops. The other mounts (Pale Acidmaw, Dauntless Duskrunner, Desire's Battle Gargon) appear to require Best Friend status.

5. **Friendship point thresholds:** The Friend Faction system uses 8,400 points per tier (42,000 total to Best Friend). However, exact friendship point amounts earned per Ember Court session per happiness level could not be verified from available sources.

6. **Dauntless Duskrunner dual source:** This mount can drop from both Mikanikos and Polemarch Adrestes. In data.js, only Mikanikos has the mount entry -- this is a reasonable simplification for the collection tracker.

7. **Loot table verification method:** All transmog item lists in this document were cross-referenced against the Wowhead loot tables for each guest's Extravagant Tribute object (fetched 2026-02-20). These loot tables are crowd-sourced from player submissions to Wowhead.
